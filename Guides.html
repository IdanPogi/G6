Default Keyboard Map

In Valorant, the default keyboard map consists of various key bindings for different actions and abilities. Here's a basic overview of the default keyboard map in Valorant:

Movement:

  • W: Move forward
  • A: Move left
  • S: Move backward
  • D: Move right
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • Ctrl: Crouch
  • Shift: Walk (to move silently)

Weapon Controls

  • 1: Equip primary weapon
  • 2: Equip secondary weapon
  • 3: Equip melee weapon
  • 4: Equip ability 1
  • 5: Equip ability 2

Abilities

  • C: Equip ultimate ability Abilities:
  • Q: Use ability 1 (agent-specific ability)
  • E: Use ability 2 (agent-specific ability)
  • X: Use ultimate ability (agent-specific ability)
  • F: Use utility ability (agent-specific ability)

    Communication:

  • B: Buy menu
  • Y: Open team chat
  • U: Open party chat
  • J: Open all chat
  • K: Open scoreboard

    Menu and Miscellaneous

  • Tab: Toggle the scoreboard
  • Esc: Open game menu (settings, exit, etc.) Please note that these key bindings can be customized in the game's settings according to your preferences. It's always a good idea to check the settings and adjust them to your liking.
