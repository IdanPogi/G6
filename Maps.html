Ascent Map


Ascent is a map that features a large central area with two bombsites located on either side. The attackers spawn on one side of the map, while the defenders spawn on the other. The central area of the map is a wide-open space that offers multiple paths to the bombsites. There are also several one-way doors that can be used to surprise the enemy.

















Bind Map


Bind is a relatively small map that is perfect for close-range combat. The map features two bombsites located on either side. The attackers and defenders spawn at the opposite end of the map. The central area of the map is a hallway that connects the two bombsites. There are also two teleporters on the map, one in the attacker spawn and one in the defender spawn.

















Haven Map


Haven is a larger map that features three bombsites, making it more challenging to defend. The attackers spawn on one side of the map, while the defenders spawn on the other. The bombsites are spread out across the map, with one in the center and the other two on either side. There are also multiple paths and chokepoints that players must navigate to reach the bombsites.

















Icebox Map


Icebox is a unique map that features a lot of verticality. The map is set in an arctic research facility and features two bombsites. The attackers spawn on one side of the map, while the defenders spawn on the other. The central area of the map is a large container area with multiple levels and platforms. There are also several ziplines that can be used to quickly move around the map.

















Breeze Map


Breeze is a VALORANT map that features a tropical island setting. The map is relatively large and features two bombsites. The attackers spawn on one side of the map, while the defenders spawn on the other. The central area of the map is a large courtyard with multiple paths to the bombsites. There are also several one-way doors and a zipline that can be used to surprise the enemy.

















Fracture Map


Fracture is a big map that features two bombsites located on either side. The attackers can spawn on any side of the map while the defenders spawn in the middle. The attackers can move freely through the edges of the map while the defenders must secure the two entries of every site.

















Pearl Map


Pearl is a relatively small map but it's perfect for long-range combats due to its long corridors. The map features two bombsites located on either side. The central area of the map has a courtyard with many corridors and alternatives paths that connects the two bombsites. The maps is perfect for lurkers and enables lots of different strategies

















Split Map


Split is a map that is divided into two distinct areas, with each team starting on one side of the map. The map features two bombsites, one on each side. The map has many paths that both attackers and defenders can use to surprise their enemies, with multiple paths leading to the bombsites. There are also several chokepoints and narrow corridors that make it challenging to navigate.
